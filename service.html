Cleaning Service

At Twinkle NYC , we care about our customers and we understand how important it is for our clients to have a clean place to live with the people you love. We use EPA product recommended by the CDC in order to protect our clients with the best products.

disinfection Service

In these difficult days, we protect our clients with the best EPA products. With this service we first clean your place with a regular cleaning or deep cleaning as you may prefer, then we start to disinfect in places where we think it is going to be in touch with people. Those places include, but are not limited to; door knob, counter, table, toilet, switches, handles, etc.

Deep cleaning

Our goal is to keep your place immaculate. With this service we provide regular cleaning and also those places that you may have forgotten. As the title says, we clean deeper and more detailed. For example, if in the regular cleaning service we clean the outside of fridge and cabinets, in this service, we clean it from inside too.

Post-Construction Cleaning

We know how difficult and tedious this type of cleaning can be. It is better to contact a professional for a post-construction clean-up. We will make your place shine.
